LinkedIn Notice

Fairly recently, I voluntarily stepped away from full-time work. After some time traveling and reflecting, I decided to tackle a long-held, bucket-list software engineering project: I’m currently building a virtual Alpha platform.

 

This platform is being developed to run on any architecture supported by the Qt framework and will be released as open source. The goal is to support OpenVMS, Tru64, and Alpha-Linux — platforms that have shaped much of my career and that I still deeply admire.

 

Having spent decades as a Digital–Oracle Rdb and CODASYL DBMS DBA, systems administrator, and software engineer (developing interfaces to and from OpenVMS databases and systems), I’ve been fortunate to work with incredible clients, colleagues, and online communities. This project is my way of giving back — and of helping to raise awareness of these remarkable technologies and the brilliant engineering behind them.

On a personal note, this journey has been rewarding and humbling. It has only deepened my admiration for the visionary teams at Digital Equipment Corporation whose work continues to inspire.

 

-- Target initial release: January 1, 2026

 

If you’re an engineering enthusiast who would like to contribute, collaborate, or simply connect, I’d be delighted to hear from you. Please feel free to reach out!
